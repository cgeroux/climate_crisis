What can I do?

It can be very difficult to know how or what you can do to make meaningful change to adress the climate crisis. I have been thinking and searching for ways I can make a difference and I am still searching. One meaningful way to take action is to talk about the climate crisis. Extended the idea of talking about the climate crisis, I have decided to put up posters to help encourage that conversation more broadly. My hope is that posters will make the issue more visible in the real world and encourage people to talk about it, reducing the taboo around the topic. There are many ways to take action and I have found that finding ways that fit into my everyday life helped me take at least some small actions. The David Suzuki foundation has a list of ways you can take action and information about how to have climate conversations which aren't always easy.
If you would like to put up posters in your local area feel free to download, modify, and or print copies of this poster to put up in your area. The poster is licensed by CC0 1.0 Universal.
This poster was created using the free and open source vector graphics program Inkscape which can be used if you wish to modify the poster. A scalar vector graphic (svg) file of the poster is available here: climate_crisis.svg which is best if you want to modify it. If you just want to print the poster as it is, this pdf file is likely what you want.
